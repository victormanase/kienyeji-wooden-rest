About Kienyeji Wooden Rest

Discover the story behind your wooden escape in the heart of Tanzania's safari country

Our Story

Welcome to Kienyeji Wooden Rest, where tradition meets comfort in the heart of Arusha, Tanzania. The name "Kienyeji" comes from Swahili, meaning "traditional" or "local," reflecting our commitment to authentic Tanzanian hospitality blended with modern comfort.

Our beautiful villa was designed as a wooden sanctuary, embracing the natural beauty and warmth that wood brings to any space. Every corner of our property tells a story of craftsmanship, attention to detail, and love for the rustic elegance that defines the Tanzanian experience.

What started as a vision to create a peaceful retreat has evolved into a beloved destination for travelers seeking both comfort and authenticity. Whether you're here for a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or preparing for the adventure of a lifetime in Tanzania's national parks, Kienyeji Wooden Rest offers the perfect blend of tranquility and convenience.

"At Kienyeji Wooden Rest, we don't just provide accommodation – we create memories that last a lifetime."

Why "Wooden Rest"?

Wood represents more than just building material to us – it symbolizes warmth, natural beauty, and connection to the earth. In Tanzania, where the baobab trees stand as ancient guardians of the landscape, we've embraced wood as the foundation of our design philosophy.

Every piece of wood in our villa has been carefully selected and crafted to create an atmosphere of rustic elegance. From the handcrafted furniture to the wooden accents throughout the property, we've created a space that feels both luxurious and authentically African.

The "Rest" in our name speaks to our promise – that here, among the wooden walls and under the Tanzanian sky, you'll find true peace and relaxation. It's your sanctuary before and after your adventures in the wild.

Arusha: Gateway to Tanzania's Wonders

Strategically located in Arusha, Tanzania's safari capital, Kienyeji Wooden Rest serves as your perfect base for exploring some of the world's most spectacular natural wonders. Our location offers you easy access to:

Mount Kilimanjaro

Africa's highest peak, just 60 km away – perfect for day trips or climbing expeditions

Serengeti National Park

Home to the Great Migration and endless wildlife viewing opportunities

Ngorongoro Crater

The world's largest unbroken volcanic caldera, a UNESCO World Heritage Site

Tarangire National Park

Famous for its ancient baobab trees and large elephant herds

Lake Manyara

Known for its flamingos and tree-climbing lions

Arusha City

Vibrant local markets, cultural sites, and authentic Tanzanian experiences
