Villa Policies & Terms

Please review our policies and terms to ensure a smooth and comfortable stay at our villa

Booking & Payment Policy

  • Payment Requirement: Full payment is required in advance before check-in
  • Accepted Payment Methods: Bank transfer, mobile money (M-Pesa, Airtel Money), cash payments
  • Rate Validity: All rates are net and inclusive of government taxes
  • Currency: Rates quoted in USD but local currency (TZS) accepted at current exchange rate
  • Confirmation: Booking is confirmed only upon receipt of full payment
  • Rate Changes: Rates are subject to change without notice until booking is confirmed

Payment Instructions

Bank transfer details and payment instructions will be provided upon booking confirmation. Please contact us directly for secure payment arrangements.

Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellation Fee: 30% of total booking amount
  • Refund Processing: Refunds processed within 7-14 business days
  • No-Show Policy: Full charges apply for no-show without prior notice
  • Force Majeure: Cancellations due to natural disasters or government restrictions may receive special consideration
  • Modification Charges: Date changes subject to availability and may incur additional charges
  • Group Bookings: Special cancellation terms apply to groups of 5+ people

Children Policy

  • Age Range: Children policy applies to ages 2-12 years
  • Sharing with Parents: Children 2-12 years pay 50% when sharing room with parents
  • Own Room: Children requiring their own room pay 80% of adult rate
  • Infants (Under 2): Stay free of charge when sharing with parents
  • Extra Bed: Additional charges apply for extra beds for children
  • Child Safety: Parents are responsible for child supervision at all times
  • Special Requirements: Please inform us of any special needs for children during booking

Check-in & Check-out Policy

  • Check-in Time: 2:00 PM - 10:00 PM
  • Check-out Time: Before 11:00 AM
  • Early Check-in: Available subject to room availability (may incur additional charges)
  • Late Check-out: Available until 2:00 PM for 50% of daily rate (subject to availability)
  • Late Arrival: Please notify us if arriving after 8:00 PM
  • Identification: Valid passport/ID required for all guests at check-in
  • Key Deposit: Refundable key deposit of $20 per room required

Villa Rules & Regulations

  • Quiet Hours: 10:00 PM - 7:00 AM for the comfort of all guests
  • Smoking Policy: Smoking only permitted in designated outdoor areas
  • Pet Policy: Pets not permitted unless pre-approved by management
  • Visitor Policy: Day visitors welcome until 9:00 PM with prior notification
  • Parties & Events: Private parties subject to approval and additional charges
  • Alcohol Policy: Responsible consumption only; no excessive drinking on premises
  • Property Care: Guests responsible for any damages to villa property
  • Security: Guests responsible for their personal belongings

Kitchen Usage Policy

  • Usage Hours: Kitchen available 24/7 for authorized guests
  • Cleanliness: Please clean up after use and wash all dishes
  • Equipment Care: Use kitchen equipment responsibly; report any damages immediately
  • Food Storage: Label personal food items with name and date
  • Grocery Shopping: Assistance available for grocery shopping (additional charge)
  • Cooking Services: Chef services available on request for special occasions
  • Safety: Turn off all appliances and gas after use

Liability & Insurance

  • Guest Responsibility: Guests stay at their own risk
  • Personal Belongings: Villa not responsible for loss or theft of personal items
  • Travel Insurance: Guests strongly advised to obtain comprehensive travel insurance
  • Medical Emergencies: Guests responsible for their own medical expenses
  • Activity Risks: Villa not liable for injuries during external activities/tours
  • Property Damage: Guests liable for any intentional or negligent damage to property
  • Force Majeure: Villa not liable for events beyond our control

Special Conditions & Services

  • Airport Transfers: Available on request at additional charge
  • Laundry Services: Available at additional cost or self-service facilities provided
  • Internet Access: Complimentary Wi-Fi throughout the property
  • Power Outages: Backup generator available for essential services
  • Water Supply: Backup water storage ensures continuous supply
  • Housekeeping: Daily housekeeping included for all bookings
  • Special Dietary Requirements: Please inform us in advance for special arrangements
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Please respect local customs and traditions
