Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a booking?

You can book through our contact form, WhatsApp, phone call, or email. We'll confirm availability and send you booking details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, mobile money, and cash payments. Full payment is required in advance as per our policy.

Do you provide airport transfers?

Yes, we can arrange airport transfers at an additional cost. Please mention this when booking.

Is Wi-Fi available?

Yes, we provide complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi throughout the property.

Can you help with safari bookings?

Absolutely! We can help arrange safari tours to Serengeti, Ngorongoro, and other national parks.

What's your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation fee is 30% of the total booking amount. See our policies page for full details.
